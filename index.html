🗑️ Delete Your YourAppName Account

This page explains how users of YourAppName, an e-commerce shopping app developed by YourDeveloperName, can request permanent deletion of their account and all associated data.

📱 How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account in 2 ways:

Option 1 — From Inside the App

Step 1: Open the YourAppName app
Step 2: Go to Profile → Settings
Step 3: Tap "Delete Account"
Step 4: Confirm by tapping "Yes, Delete My Account"
Step 5: Your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days

Option 2 — Via Email Request

Step 1: Send an email to support@yourapp.com
Step 2: Use subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
Step 3: Include your registered email address in the message
Step 4: We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email

📊 Data Deletion Details

Here is exactly what happens to your data when you delete your account:

Data Type Action Timeframe
Name, Email, Phone Number ✅ Permanently Deleted Within 30 days
Delivery Address & Profile ✅ Permanently Deleted Within 30 days
Wishlist & Cart Items ✅ Permanently Deleted Within 30 days
Product Reviews & Ratings ✅ Permanently Deleted Within 30 days
Order History ⚠️ Retained (Legal Requirement) 7 years (tax/legal)
Payment Transaction Records ⚠️ Retained (Legal Requirement) 7 years (tax/legal)
App Usage & Analytics Logs ⚠️ Anonymized 90 days
⚠️ Important: Order history and payment records are retained for legal and tax compliance purposes only. This data will NOT be used for marketing after account deletion.
